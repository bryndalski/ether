What makes us distinctive

A precision instrument, not a dashboard.

Deep OLED floor that reads as empty until something is on. The one thing that glows is the brand — a heat gradient (magenta → red-orange → amber, "a curling iron heating up"), reserved for exactly four things: Send, active / progress, the waterfall wait / download phases, and the live env accent. Everything else stays quiet, dense, monospaced-where-it-counts neutral ink. Color always means something — a method, a status, a phase — never decorates.
